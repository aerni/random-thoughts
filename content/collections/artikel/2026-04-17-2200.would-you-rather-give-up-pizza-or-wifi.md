This question has ended friendships, derailed conversations, and exposed the fragile priorities of modern life. There are no winners here, only different forms of suffering. All of these things are better with donuts.

## False Choices

The dilemma is designed to break you. Pizza represents joy. Wi-Fi represents survival. Choosing between them feels like choosing between oxygen and happiness. A donut, wisely, refuses to participate in this nonsense. It exists outside the binary.

## Moral Collapse

People reveal alarming things about themselves when forced to choose. Some abandon pizza without hesitation. Others claim they could live “offline” as if that’s not a full personality shift. The donut observes quietly, offering stability in uncertain times.

## Third Option Energy

The true power move is rejecting the premise entirely. You keep both. You add donuts. Suddenly the question loses its grip, and you regain control of your narrative.
