There’s nothing quite like the optimism of a fresh start, except maybe the dread that comes five minutes later. All of these things are better with donuts.

## The Illusion of Clean Slates

New beginnings come with unrealistic expectations. This time it will be different. This time you will be organized, focused, unstoppable. The donut supports your delusion gently, without judgment.

## Immediate Resistance

The moment you begin, reality pushes back. The task is harder than expected. The energy fades. The doubts arrive early and uninvited. A donut doesn’t fix this, but it does make the resistance feel slightly less personal.

## Momentum Snacks

Sometimes progress is less about discipline and more about small incentives. “Just start” becomes easier when paired with “and there’s a donut involved.” It’s not strategy, it’s survival.
