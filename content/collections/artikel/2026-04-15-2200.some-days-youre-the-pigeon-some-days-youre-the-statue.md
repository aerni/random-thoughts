Life has a way of balancing things out in the most humbling ways. All of these things are better with donuts.

## Unexpected Roles

One day you’re thriving. The next, you’re wondering how things went so wrong so quickly. The transition is rarely explained.

## Humility Cycles

Being the pigeon feels powerful. Being the statue builds character. Neither state is permanent, which is both comforting and deeply inconvenient.

## Neutral Ground

A donut does not care which role you’re currently playing. It treats both pigeons and statues with equal respect. This is fairness at its finest.
